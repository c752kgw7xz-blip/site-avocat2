About

Graduated with a law degree (Judicial Careers) from the University of Bordeaux in 1986 and a certificate in criminal sciences, Me ALBERTIN-ROBY was admitted to the bar on 1 January 1991 and has practiced at the Bar of Nice for over thirty-three years.

Member of Divorce France, the Association of Family Law Practitioners at the Bar of Nice (child advocate), and M.I.A (Real Estate Market of Lawyers). Authorized for real estate transactions.
