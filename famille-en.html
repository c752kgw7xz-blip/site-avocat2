PROCEDURAL REPRESENTATION

The Firm assists and represents you in all family law proceedings, notably regarding the 2021 divorce reform and mutual-consent divorces by lawyer's act.

The Firm also handles liquidation of matrimonial regimes in cooperation with notaries, post-divorce requests (revision of compensatory allowances, modification of parental authority), and drafting or signing of lawyer acts (parental agreements).

Typical case progress

1) Situation analysis

Listening, verification of elements, identification of issues (personal, patrimonial, children), and framing of possible options.

2) Strategy & implementation

Drafting, negotiation, acts and/or proceedings according to the context, followed by monitoring until agreement or decision.

Priority: proportionate solutions

When an agreement is possible and protects your interests, it is sought. When proceedings are necessary, they are conducted with a clear strategy.
