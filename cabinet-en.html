The firm

A clear, rigorous and adapted approach

COACHING

The firm focuses on pre-litigation coaching to help you make informed decisions about separation procedures.

We support you to try to ease conflict and avoid contentious proceedings through six one-hour sessions (two sessions per month), seeking an amicable solution before litigation.

We assist in finding solutions regarding personal consequences of separation (the couple and, above all, the children), financial consequences (child support, compensatory allowance) and the dissolution of matrimonial regimes.

Package: €120 per session (the sixth session is free).
